Long Ladder

In-official training task.

Helps getting warmed up, as launches get more close to each other at the end.

Start with 75 seconds, and decrease each next target with 5 seconds.
15 targets, of which last two are not possible to reach.

Working time is 10 minutes.

Maximum possible score is 10:00 - 14s = 9:46, making the last flight start only 1s before end of working time.
